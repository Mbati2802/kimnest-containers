## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Project Overview

KimNest Containers (K) is an innovative company specializing in container sales, fabrication, modification, and customized container solutions. The company transforms ordinary shipping containers into functional, attractive, and durable spaces for residential, commercial, institutional, and industrial use.

This proposal outlines the recommended website structure, user experience, content strategy, and information architecture that will position KimNest Containers as one of Kenya's leading container fabrication companies.

The website will serve as a powerful marketing and lead generation platform designed to showcase the company's expertise, completed projects, product range, and customer-focused solutions while making it easy for potential clients to request quotations and engage with the business.

Rather than simply displaying products, the website will tell the KimNest story, demonstrate craftsmanship, inspire customers through completed projects, and guide visitors toward making an enquiry or requesting a quotation.

## 11. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. **Do you sell both new and used shipping containers?** Yes. We supply both new and used shipping containers depending on your requirements, budget, and intended application.

2. **Can you customize containers?** Absolutely. We specialize in custom fabrication, including offices, homes, shops, restaurants, classrooms, clinics, and other modular structures.

3. **Do you deliver across Kenya?** Yes. We provide nationwide delivery and professional installation services.

4. **How long does fabrication take?** Project timelines depend on the size and complexity of the work. Once your requirements are confirmed, we'll provide an estimated completion schedule.

5. **Can I provide my own design?** Yes. We can fabricate based on your architectural drawings or collaborate with you to develop a custom design.

6. **Are container homes durable?** Yes. Shipping containers are built from high-strength steel and are designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making them an excellent long-term building solution.

7. **Do container buildings require planning approvals?** Depending on your location and intended use, approvals may be required. We recommend consulting the relevant local authorities before construction.

8. **Can containers be insulated?** Yes. We offer insulation solutions that improve thermal comfort and energy efficiency.

9. **Do you install electrical and plumbing systems?** Yes. We provide complete electrical, plumbing, lighting, and interior finishing services.

10. **Can containers have multiple rooms?** Certainly. Through professional partitioning and design, containers can accommodate multiple functional spaces.

11. **Can containers be stacked?** Yes. Containers are structurally designed to support stacking, making them ideal for multi-storey developments.

12. **Do you provide after-sales support?** Yes. We remain available to assist clients with maintenance advice, modifications, and future expansion projects.

13. **Can I visit your workshop?** Yes. Clients are welcome to schedule visits to view ongoing and completed fabrication projects.

14. **How do I get a quotation?** Simply complete the online quotation form, contact us by phone, or send us a WhatsApp message with your project requirements.

15. **Why choose KimNest Containers?** Because we combine innovative design, quality craftsmanship, professional service, competitive pricing, and customized solutions to deliver projects that exceed expectations.
